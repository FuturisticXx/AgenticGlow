# AgenticGlow Product Context

## Product register

Product UI

## Users

Mac users who run Codex, Claude Code, and related AI development tools and want a private, glanceable view of session activity and allowance status.

## Product purpose

AgenticGlow makes local AI activity understandable at a glance through a menu bar app and widgets, without turning that activity into a noisy analytics dashboard.

## Brand personality

Calm, native, quietly expressive, trustworthy, and precise.

## Anti-references

- No neon or gamer styling
- No decorative glass effects that reduce legibility
- No fake urgency or alarming states for routine activity
- No dense dashboards when a concise status is enough
- No low-contrast text or motion that competes with the data

## Design principles

- Make the current state understandable in one glance.
- Show honest status and uncertainty instead of inventing precision.
- Prefer native macOS behavior and system materials.
- Use color and motion sparingly, with a clear semantic purpose.
- Keep local privacy visible in the product experience.

## Accessibility and inclusion

Follow Apple Human Interface Guidelines for contrast, text sizing, VoiceOver, keyboard access, and reduced-motion behavior. Never rely on color alone to communicate state.
